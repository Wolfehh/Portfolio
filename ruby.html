Ruby

Ruby is a side view, bullet-hell game made using Unity. In the game you play as Ruby, a mute girl, and must defeat the monsters that have taken over the school, while looking for your brother Trevor. The gameplay centers around defeating monsters with the characters given weapon, using power-ups and monitoring your health to make it to the end. The game was a team project completed over a 14 week period at Michigan Technological University in the Husky Game Development Enterprise. I acted as the narrative designer, script writer, and programmer, as well as assisted in the general game design.
