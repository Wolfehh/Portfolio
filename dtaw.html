Delivery to Another World

Delivery To Another World is a stealth focused, roguelike game that takes place in a 3 dimensional world with 2 dimensional assets. Ethan is killed by an Amazon package and wakes up in a fantasy world where everyone is 2D. He is helped by a thieves guild to acclimate to this new world, however soon he realizes that he is the only one able to turn to face a different plane, making him invisible to everyone else, and everyone else invisible to him. Ethan will go on quests for the thieves guild, stealing items that will help him on his way to get your revenge on the king of the land who reminds him a little too much of the CEO of Amazon from our world...

The game was a team project completed over a 14 week period at Michigan Technological University in the Husky Game Development Enterprise. I acted as the teams manager, a voice actor, and provided assistance when available. I also assisted in the creation of the trailer.
