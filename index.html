About

I'm a 3rd year student at Michigan Technological University. Currently, I am working on a B.S. Computer Science degree with a concentration in Game Development and a minor in Cybersecurity. It has been a long time dream to design games and create stories that players can get lost in.

I have been a part of the Husky Game Development Enterprise at MTU for almost my entire duration of school. I have been in it as a game designer/developer, the VP of Finance, and am currently the President.
